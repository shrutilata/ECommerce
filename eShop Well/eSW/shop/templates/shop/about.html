eShop Well !

eShop Well was founded on 4 February 2010 as a daily deals platform, but expanded in September 2011 to become an online marketplace.It has grown to become one of the largest online marketplace in India.In March 2015, it brought actor Aamir Khan for the promotion of its website in India.In October 2017, eShopWell's CFO Raunit Kashyap resigned. 20 November 2019 eShop Well joined International Trademark Association (INTA) as part of its efforts to support protection of intellectual property on online marketplaces. eShop Well was mentioned in the 2020 Review of Notorious Markets for Counterfeiting and Piracy issued by the Office of the US Trade Representative. The report states that eShop Well is facilitating the sale of pirated and counterfeit goods Funding
